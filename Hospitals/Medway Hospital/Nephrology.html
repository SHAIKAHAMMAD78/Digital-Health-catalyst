Conditions

Chronic Kidney Disease

Gradual loss of renal function over time, impacting health and leading to potential kidney failure complications.

Acute Kidney Injury

Sudden, severe decline in kidney efficiency, often requiring urgent medical intervention to prevent further harm.

Kidney Stones

Kidney stones, hard deposits in kidneys, cause intense pain and urinary flow issues, necessitating medical attention and treatment.

Glomerulonephritis

Glomerulonephritis is an inflammatory kidney condition that may lead to chronic kidney failure if untreated or severe.

Polycystic Kidney Disease

Hereditary disorder characterized by numerous cysts in the kidneys, causing enlargement and reduced efficiency.

Hypertensive Nephropathy

Long-standing high blood pressure can impair kidneys, impacting renal function and overall health significantly over time.
