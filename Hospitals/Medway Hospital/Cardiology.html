About the Speciality

Specializing in the intricate domain of cardiology, this medical discipline focuses on safeguarding the well-being of the heart and vascular system. Through a comprehensive array of treatments and preventative strategies, heart specialist hospital in chennai aims to address an array of heart-related ailments, ensuring the maintenance of optimal cardiovascular health.

From managing existing conditions to implementing proactive measures, the overarching goal remains the promotion of longevity and quality of life through the preservation of cardiac function and circulatory efficiency.




Conditions

Coronary Artery Disease

Blockages or narrowing in the coronary arteries reduce blood flow, affecting heart function and increasing heart attack risk.

Heart Arrhythmias

Irregular heart rhythms, from too fast to too slow, can disrupt blood flow and lead to significant health issues if not managed.

Heart Failure

A progressive condition where the heart weakens, losing its ability to pump blood efficiently, leading to fluid buildup and fatigue.

Hypertension

Elevated blood pressure over time can cause serious damage to heart and arterial health, leading to heart disease and stroke risks.

Valvular Heart Disease

Involves one or more of the heart's valves, impairing the flow of blood through the heart, which can necessitate surgical intervention.

Congenital Heart Defects

Structural heart defects present from birth that can alter the normal flow of blood through the heart, often requiring surgical repair.
