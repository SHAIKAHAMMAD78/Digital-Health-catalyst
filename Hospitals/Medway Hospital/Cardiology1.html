Cardiology

Cardiology is the medical specialty focused on diagnosing and treating conditions related to the heart and blood vessels. This field encompasses a wide range of disorders, including coronary artery disease, heart rhythm disorders, and heart failure. The growth of cardiology in Chennai has led to improved access to advanced heart care for patients throughout the region. Cardiologists are skilled in various diagnostic techniques, such as echocardiograms and stress tests, and provide both medical and interventional treatments to manage heart conditions effectively. Their expertise in cardiology is crucial in preventing and managing heart-related diseases, ensuring patients lead healthier lives.
