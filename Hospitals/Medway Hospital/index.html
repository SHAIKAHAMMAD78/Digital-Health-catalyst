Our Specialties

Our medical team is comprised of some of the finest doctors in the region, renowned for their clinical expertise and innovative approaches to medicine.

Pediatrics

Pediatrics focuses on caring for infants, children, and adolescents’ overall health and well-being.

Bio-Chemistry

Biochemistry examines chemical processes in organisms, including metabolism, enzymes, and DNA.

Cardiothoracic Surgery

Cardiothoracic surgery is a specialized field that addresses conditions affecting the heart and lungs.

Neurology

Neurology focuses on disorders of the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and nerves.

Orthopedics

Orthopedics specializes in musculoskeletal issues, including bone, joint, and muscle conditions.
