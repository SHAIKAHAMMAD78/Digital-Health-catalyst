Skin Conditions We Treat

Chemical Peels

Chemical peels rejuvenate the skin by exfoliating the outer layer, improving texture, reducing pigmentation, and treating acne scars. This treatment enhances skin clarity and radiance for a youthful appearance.

Injectables (Botox & Fillers)

Botox smoothens fine lines and wrinkles, while dermal fillers restore lost volume and enhance facial contours. These non-surgical treatments provide a refreshed, youthful look.

Lasers

Laser treatments effectively address pigmentation, scars, and unwanted hair. They offer precision and long-lasting results, improving skin texture and tone.

Microneedling RF

Microneedling RF stimulates collagen production to tighten skin and reduce scars, wrinkles, and stretch marks. It’s a minimally invasive treatment for skin rejuvenation.

Dermatosurgery

Dermatosurgery involves minor skin surgeries to treat lesions, cysts, or warts, ensuring functional and cosmetic improvement with minimal scarring.

Cosmetic Procedures

Cosmetic dermatology includes treatments like facelifts, skin tightening, and anti-ageing therapies, enhancing aesthetic appeal and boosting confidence.
