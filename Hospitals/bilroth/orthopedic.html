Comprehensive Musculoskeletal Care

At Bilroth Hospital's Orthopedic Department, we provide exceptional care for all musculoskeletal conditions. Our team of board-certified orthopedic surgeons and specialists are dedicated to helping patients regain mobility and live pain-free lives.

Our department is equipped with the latest technology including robotic-assisted surgery systems, advanced imaging equipment, and a state-of-the-art rehabilitation center. We specialize in both conservative treatments and advanced surgical interventions.

We take a patient-centered approach, developing personalized treatment plans that may include physical therapy, medication, minimally invasive procedures, or complex reconstructive surgeries.
