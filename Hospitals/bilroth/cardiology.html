Advanced Cardiac Care at Bilroth

At Bilroth Hospital's Cardiology Department, we are committed to providing comprehensive, state-of-the-art cardiac care to our patients. Our team of board-certified cardiologists and cardiac surgeons bring together decades of experience in diagnosing, treating, and preventing heart diseases.

Our department is equipped with the latest diagnostic and therapeutic technologies, including advanced cardiac catheterization labs, 3D echocardiography, and cutting-edge electrophysiology equipment.

We follow a patient-centered approach, ensuring personalized treatment plans tailored to each individual's unique needs and health goals.

Our Cardiac Services

Comprehensive care for all heart conditions

Preventive Cardiology

Comprehensive risk assessment and prevention strategies for heart disease including lifestyle modification programs.

Interventional Cardiology

Advanced procedures including angioplasty, stenting, and transcatheter valve replacements.

Cardiac Surgery

Minimally invasive and traditional open-heart surgeries performed by our expert cardiac surgeons.

Electrophysiology

Diagnosis and treatment of heart rhythm disorders including ablation and pacemaker implantation.

Heart Failure Program

Comprehensive management including advanced therapies like ventricular assist devices.

Pediatric Cardiology

Specialized care for children with congenital and acquired heart conditions.
