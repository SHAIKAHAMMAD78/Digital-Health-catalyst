Comprehensive Neurological Care

At Bilroth Hospital's Neurology Department, we provide world-class care for disorders of the nervous system. Our team of board-certified neurologists and neurosurgeons combine extensive clinical experience with the latest advancements in neurological medicine.

Our department features state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment including 3T MRI, CT angiography, EEG, EMG, and advanced neurophysiology labs. We specialize in both medical and surgical management of neurological conditions.

We take a multidisciplinary approach to neurological care, collaborating with specialists across departments to develop comprehensive treatment plans tailored to each patient's needs.

Our Neurological Services

Comprehensive care for all neurological conditions

Stroke Care

24/7 stroke intervention with advanced thrombolysis and thrombectomy procedures in our certified stroke center.

Epilepsy Management

Comprehensive epilepsy program including video EEG monitoring, medication management, and surgical options.

Movement Disorders

Specialized care for Parkinson's disease, essential tremor, dystonia and other movement disorders including DBS.

Memory Disorders

Diagnosis and management of Alzheimer's disease, dementia, and other cognitive disorders.

Neurodegenerative Diseases

Comprehensive care for ALS, multiple sclerosis, and other neurodegenerative conditions.

Neurosurgery

Advanced surgical interventions for brain tumors, aneurysms, spinal disorders, and functional neurosurgery.
