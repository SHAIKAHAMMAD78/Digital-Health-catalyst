Surgery Overview

General surgery focuses on abdominal organs, breast, skin, soft tissues, and trauma. Surgeons perform both emergency and elective procedures using various techniques.

Common Procedures

  • Appendectomy: Appendix removal
  • Cholecystectomy: Gallbladder removal
  • Hernia Repair: Abdominal wall correction
  • Mastectomy: Breast tissue removal
  • Bowel Resection: Intestinal surgery
  • Biopsies: Tissue sample collection

Surgical Approaches

Modern surgery utilizes different techniques:

  1. Open Surgery: Traditional large incision
  2. Laparoscopic: Minimally invasive with small incisions
  3. Robotic-Assisted: Precision surgery using robotic systems
