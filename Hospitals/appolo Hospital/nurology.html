Neurology Overview

Neurology focuses on disorders of the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and nerves. Neurologists diagnose and treat conditions affecting these systems.

Key Conditions

  • Stroke: Brain blood supply interruption
  • Epilepsy: Recurrent seizures
  • Alzheimer's: Progressive memory loss
  • Parkinson's: Movement disorder
  • Migraines: Severe headaches
  • MS: Central nervous system autoimmune disease

Brain Basics

The brain controls all body functions through three main parts:

  1. Cerebrum: Thought and action
  2. Cerebellum: Movement coordination
  3. Brainstem: Basic life functions
