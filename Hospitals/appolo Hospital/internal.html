Internal Medicine Overview

Internal medicine physicians specialize in preventing, diagnosing, and treating adult diseases. They manage complex medical conditions and coordinate patient care.

Patient Consultation
Physical Examination
Medical Research

Common Conditions Treated

  • Diabetes: Blood sugar management
  • Hypertension: High blood pressure control
  • Heart Disease: Cardiovascular conditions
  • Respiratory Disorders: Asthma, COPD
  • Infectious Diseases: Bacterial/viral infections
  • Metabolic Disorders: Thyroid, cholesterol

Diagnostic Approaches

Internists use various methods to diagnose health issues:

  1. Physical Exams: Comprehensive patient assessment
  2. Lab Tests: Blood work and urinalysis
  3. Imaging: X-rays, CT scans, and MRIs
  4. Medical History: Detailed patient background
