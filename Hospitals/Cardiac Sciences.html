Cardiac Sciences

Overview - Heart Hospital

Cardiac Science is a medical field focused on diagnosing, treating, and preventing heart diseases. The treatment procedures under cardiac science include emergency heart care, angioplasty, bypass surgery, and heart transplants. These can be found at Cardiac Hospitals that offer specialised treatment. The goal of medical professionals in cardiac science is to improve heart health and patient outcomes through personalised treatment plans and cutting-edge procedures. By choosing renowned heart hospitals like SPARSH, patients can receive specialised attention from some of the best cardiologists in Bangalore and access to the latest advancements in cardiac care, ensuring the best possible recovery and quality of life.

Multidisciplinary Treatment Approach

SPARSH is a well-known heart hospital that works on a multidisciplinary approach. We have a team of the best cardiac specialists in Bangalore. Our Cardiac Team of cardiologists, cardiac surgeons, anesthesiologists & intensivists, collaborate with each other to provide the best possible outcome for the given patient. Patients are also supported equally by other specialists like nephrologists, pulmonologists, and neurologists, as most often, diseases require care from multiple specialists. Our team evaluates the patients and works together to provide the best treatment outcomes possible. This collaborative approach establishes us as a premier heart hospital.

Cardiac Surgery

SPARSH is well known for cardiac surgery. The cardiac surgery here involves advanced procedures to treat conditions like coronary artery disease, heart valve issues, and congenital defects. We are a premier hospital for heart surgery because of the state-of-the-art facilities available. Patients benefit from minimally invasive techniques, reducing recovery times and enhancing outcomes. By choosing SPARSH Hospitals, patients can receive comprehensive care from skilled surgeons using the latest technology, ensuring the best possible treatment for complex heart conditions.
