About Us

In the year 2009 in suburban of Mumbai a new hospital namely Raksha Hospital. Beginning with initial bed strength of a mere 20 beds, Raksha Hospital has today grown by leaps and bounds, into bigger and best hospitals in the suburbs. Today Raksha Hospital has expanded into a hi-tech 40 bedded hospital with all the advanced diagnostics facilities, top class medical care, the best of doctors, medical equipment, paramedical staff, quality service and plenty of medical experience and expertise. The hospital is situated in one of the prime location in Malad (West) i.e. Marve Charkop Junction.The hospital working with all qualified staffs with dedication, determination, proper hard work of Staff to provide the best services to all the patients. The Hospital having its own pathology lab, X-Ray Facility with in-house pharmacy & 24 hrs emergency ambulance service, 24 hrs CCTV surveillance with 10 days recording facility which is also connected to the internet FREE wifi connectivity.
