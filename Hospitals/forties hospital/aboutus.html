About Forties Hospital

Compassionate care, advanced medicine, close to home

Our Story

Founded in 1985, Forties Hospital has grown from a small community hospital to a leading healthcare provider in the region. Our journey has been guided by a commitment to excellence in patient care, medical innovation, and community service.

Today, we stand as a 350-bed multi-specialty hospital with state-of-the-art facilities and a team of over 200 highly skilled doctors. Our hospital is accredited by the Joint Commission International (JCI), reflecting our dedication to maintaining the highest standards of healthcare quality and patient safety.

At Forties Hospital, we believe that healthcare should be accessible, compassionate, and tailored to meet the unique needs of each patient. Our patient-centered approach has earned us numerous awards and recognition as one of the top hospitals in the country.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional healthcare services through advanced technology, skilled professionals, and compassionate care, improving the quality of life for our patients and the communities we serve.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a world-class healthcare institution that sets the standard for excellence in patient care, medical education, and research while maintaining our commitment to ethical practices and community service.

Our Values

Compassion, Excellence, Integrity, Innovation, and Teamwork guide every decision we make and every action we take in serving our patients and community.
