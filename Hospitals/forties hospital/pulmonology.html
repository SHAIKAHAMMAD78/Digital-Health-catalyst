Diagnostic & Treatment Services

Pulmonary Function Tests

Comprehensive breathing tests to measure lung capacity, airflow, and gas exchange.

Bronchoscopy

Minimally invasive procedure to examine airways and collect tissue samples.

CT Scans & X-rays

Advanced imaging for detailed views of lung structure and abnormalities.

Sleep Studies

Overnight monitoring to diagnose sleep apnea and other breathing disorders.

Medication Management

Personalized treatment plans with inhalers, biologics, and other therapies.

Oxygen Therapy

Supplemental oxygen for patients with chronic breathing difficulties.

Pulmonary Rehabilitation

Supervised exercise and education program to improve lung function.

Minimally Invasive Procedures

Thoracentesis, bronchial thermoplasty, and other advanced interventions.
