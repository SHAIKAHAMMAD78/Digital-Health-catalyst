Stool Culture Test

What is a Stool Culture Test?

A stool culture test is used to detect bacteria or infections in stool samples to diagnose digestive issues.

How Does It Work?

A stool sample is collected and analyzed in a lab to identify harmful bacteria or infections.

Why is It Important?

It helps diagnose bacterial infections, food poisoning, and digestive disorders.

Benefits of Stool Culture Test

Provides accurate diagnosis of gastrointestinal infections, leading to effective treatment.

How to Prepare

Avoiding certain foods and medications before the test ensures accurate results.

Understanding Results

Results identify bacteria or infections present, guiding proper treatment plans.
