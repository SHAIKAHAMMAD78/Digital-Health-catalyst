Diffusion Capacity Test (DLCO)

What is the DLCO Test?

The Diffusion Capacity Test (DLCO) measures how well oxygen passes from the lungs into the blood.

How Does DLCO Work?

Patients inhale a small amount of carbon monoxide, and the test measures how much is absorbed into the bloodstream.

Why is the DLCO Test Important?

DLCO helps diagnose lung diseases such as COPD, pulmonary fibrosis, and pulmonary hypertension.

Understanding the Results

Low DLCO values may indicate lung damage, while normal values suggest good gas exchange efficiency.

How to Prepare

Avoid smoking and heavy meals before the test to ensure accurate results.

Benefits of the DLCO Test

It provides valuable insight into lung function and helps monitor the progression of respiratory diseases.
