Peak Flow Measurement

What is Peak Flow Measurement?

Peak flow measurement is a simple test to assess lung function by measuring the maximum speed of exhalation.

How is the Peak Flow Test Performed?

A person blows forcefully into a peak flow meter, which measures the highest airflow rate achieved during expiration.

Why is Peak Flow Measurement Important?

It helps monitor asthma, detect airway constriction, and assess the effectiveness of medications.

Understanding the Results

Results provide insight into lung function and can help in adjusting treatment plans based on peak flow variability.

How to Prepare

Ensure proper technique by sealing lips around the meter mouthpiece and exhaling forcefully.

Benefits of Peak Flow Measurement

Regular peak flow monitoring helps manage respiratory conditions and prevent asthma attacks.
