Vitamin and Mineral Tests

What are Vitamin and Mineral Tests?

These tests measure the levels of essential vitamins and minerals in your blood to detect deficiencies or imbalances.

How Do They Work?

A blood sample is taken and analyzed to assess levels of vitamins like D, B12, and minerals such as iron and calcium.

Why are They Important?

Vitamin and mineral deficiencies can lead to various health issues, including fatigue, weak immunity, and bone problems.

Benefits of These Tests

Regular testing helps in early diagnosis of deficiencies and allows for proper dietary and supplement adjustments.

How to Prepare

Some tests require fasting, while others may need you to avoid certain supplements before testing.

Understanding Results

Results provide insight into dietary needs and necessary interventions to maintain optimal health.
