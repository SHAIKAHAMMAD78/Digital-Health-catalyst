Basic Metabolic Panel (BMP)

What is a Basic Metabolic Panel (BMP)?

The Basic Metabolic Panel (BMP) is a blood test that provides important information about your body's metabolism, including kidney function, electrolyte levels, and blood sugar.

How Does It Work?

A blood sample is drawn and analyzed to measure levels of key substances, such as glucose, calcium, and electrolytes like sodium and potassium.

Why is It Important?

The BMP test helps diagnose conditions like kidney disease, diabetes, and electrolyte imbalances that can impact overall health.

Benefits of BMP Test

The test is a crucial tool in monitoring health conditions, managing chronic diseases, and evaluating the effectiveness of treatments.
