What is a Blood Test?

A blood test is a laboratory analysis performed on a blood sample to assess overall health and detect diseases.

Common Conditions Diagnosed

  • Anemia: Checks for low red blood cell count.
  • Infections: Identifies bacterial, viral, or fungal infections.
  • Diabetes: Measures blood sugar levels.
  • Cholesterol Levels: Determines risk for heart disease.
  • Liver & Kidney Function: Evaluates organ health.

Why is a Blood Test Performed?

  • Monitor overall health
  • Detect potential diseases early
  • Assess organ function
  • Evaluate risk factors for conditions like heart disease
