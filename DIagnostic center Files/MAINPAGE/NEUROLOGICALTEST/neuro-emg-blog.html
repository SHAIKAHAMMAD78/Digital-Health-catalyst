EMG & NCV

What are EMG & NCV Tests?

Electromyography (EMG) and Nerve Conduction Study (NCV) are diagnostic tests that evaluate muscle and nerve function.

How Does EMG Work?

EMG measures electrical activity in muscles using fine needle electrodes inserted into the muscle tissue.

How Does NCV Work?

NCV assesses nerve function by applying electrical stimulation to a nerve and measuring the response.

Why Are These Tests Important?

They help diagnose conditions like neuropathy, muscular disorders, and nerve compression syndromes.

How to Prepare

Avoid lotions on the skin before the test and inform the doctor about any blood-thinning medications.

Understanding Results

Results help determine the presence of nerve or muscle disorders and guide further treatment.
