Oral Food Challenge Test

What is an Oral Food Challenge Test?

The Oral Food Challenge (OFC) is a medically supervised test used to diagnose food allergies by gradually consuming suspected allergens.

How Does It Work?

Under medical supervision, small amounts of the suspected food are eaten in increasing doses to monitor for allergic reactions.

Why is It Important?

This test helps confirm or rule out food allergies, reducing unnecessary food avoidance and improving quality of life.

Benefits of Oral Food Challenge

Provides the most accurate food allergy diagnosis, helping individuals safely reintroduce foods they may have been avoiding.

How to Prepare

Consult with your doctor, stop antihistamines before the test, and ensure you have medical support available during testing.

Understanding Results

Results determine if a food allergy exists, guiding doctors in treatment plans and safe dietary recommendations.
