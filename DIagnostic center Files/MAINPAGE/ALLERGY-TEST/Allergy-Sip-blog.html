Skin Prick Test (SPT)

What is a Skin Prick Test?

The Skin Prick Test (SPT) is a quick and effective way to diagnose allergies. It helps identify reactions to common allergens like pollen, dust, and food.

How Does It Work?

Small amounts of allergens are placed on your skin, and a tiny prick allows them to enter. If you're allergic, a small bump will appear.

Why is SPT Important?

SPT helps doctors create personalized treatment plans, including allergy shots and avoidance strategies.

Benefits of SPT

SPT is safe, quick, and provides immediate results. It's suitable for both children and adults.

How to Prepare

Avoid antihistamines before the test. Wear comfortable clothing and inform your doctor about any medications you're taking.

Understanding Results

Results are usually available within 15-20 minutes. Your doctor will explain what they mean and recommend next steps.
