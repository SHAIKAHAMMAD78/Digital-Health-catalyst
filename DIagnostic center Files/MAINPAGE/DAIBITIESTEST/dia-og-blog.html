Oral Glucose Tolerance Test (OGTT)

What is OGTT?

The Oral Glucose Tolerance Test (OGTT) is a diagnostic test used to measure how well the body processes glucose over time.

How Does It Work?

After fasting, a person drinks a glucose solution, and blood samples are taken at intervals to monitor blood sugar response.

Why is It Important?

OGTT is used to diagnose diabetes, gestational diabetes, and insulin resistance, helping doctors manage blood sugar disorders effectively.
