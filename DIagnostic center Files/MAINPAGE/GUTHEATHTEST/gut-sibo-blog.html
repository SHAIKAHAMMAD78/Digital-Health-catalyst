SIBO Breath Test

What is a SIBO Breath Test?

The SIBO Breath Test is used to diagnose Small Intestinal Bacterial Overgrowth (SIBO) by measuring gas levels in the breath.

How Does It Work?

The test measures hydrogen and methane gas levels in your breath after consuming a sugar solution.

Why is It Important?

This test helps diagnose SIBO, which can cause bloating, diarrhea, and other digestive issues.

How to Prepare

Avoid certain foods, antibiotics, and probiotics for a specified period before the test, as advised by your doctor.

Understanding Results

The results indicate the presence of excessive bacterial growth in the small intestine, guiding treatment options.
