Lactose Intolerance Test

What is a Lactose Intolerance Test?

The Lactose Intolerance Test is used to diagnose an individual's ability to digest lactose, a sugar found in dairy products.

How Does It Work?

The test measures the body's response to lactose through a breath test, blood test, or stool acidity test.

Why is It Important?

This test helps diagnose lactose intolerance, a condition that causes digestive discomfort after consuming dairy.

How to Prepare

Fasting may be required before the test. Avoid certain foods and medications as advised by your doctor.

Understanding Results

The test determines if the body can properly digest lactose and helps guide dietary recommendations.
