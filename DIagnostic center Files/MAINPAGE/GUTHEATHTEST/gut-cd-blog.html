Celiac Disease Test

What is a Celiac Disease Test?

The Celiac Disease Test is used to diagnose celiac disease by detecting antibodies in the blood or through a biopsy of the small intestine.

How Does It Work?

The test detects specific antibodies related to celiac disease or examines intestinal tissue for damage caused by gluten consumption.

Why is It Important?

This test helps diagnose celiac disease, which can cause digestive issues, nutrient deficiencies, and other health complications if left untreated.

How to Prepare

It is important to continue eating gluten before the test to ensure accurate results. Consult with a doctor for specific instructions.

Understanding Results

The results indicate whether antibodies associated with celiac disease are present or if intestinal damage has occurred due to gluten.
