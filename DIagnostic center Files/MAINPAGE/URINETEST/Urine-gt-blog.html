Urine Glucose Test

What is a Urine Glucose Test?

A urine glucose test measures sugar levels in urine to help diagnose diabetes and other conditions.

How Does It Work?

A urine sample is tested for glucose presence, which may indicate high blood sugar levels.

Why is It Important?

This test helps monitor diabetes and detect conditions like kidney disease.

Benefits of Urine Glucose Test

It provides a quick, non-invasive way to check for abnormal glucose levels.

How to Prepare

No special preparation is required, but hydration is advised before the test.

Understanding Results

Results indicate glucose levels in urine, which can help diagnose diabetes or kidney issues.
