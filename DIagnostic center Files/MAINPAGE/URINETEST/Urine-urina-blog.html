Urinalysis (Routine Urine Test)

What is Urinalysis?

Urinalysis is a routine test that examines urine to detect signs of disease, infection, or other health conditions.

How Does It Work?

A urine sample is analyzed for color, clarity, chemical composition, and microscopic elements.

Why is It Important?

Urinalysis helps detect infections, kidney disorders, diabetes, and other health issues early.

Benefits of Urinalysis

It is a non-invasive, quick test that helps in routine health monitoring and disease prevention.

How to Prepare

Drinking plenty of water is recommended, and certain foods or medications may affect results.

Understanding Results

Results can indicate infections, kidney health, or metabolic conditions requiring further examination.
