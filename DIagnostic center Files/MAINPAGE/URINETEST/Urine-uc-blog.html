Urine Culture and Sensitivity Test

What is a Urine Culture Test?

A urine culture test detects bacterial infections in the urinary tract by identifying the specific microorganisms present.

How Does It Work?

A urine sample is incubated in a lab to promote bacterial growth, helping identify infections and guide treatment.

Why is It Important?

This test is essential for diagnosing urinary tract infections (UTIs) and determining the most effective antibiotics.

Benefits of Urine Culture

It ensures accurate infection detection and helps prevent antibiotic resistance by identifying the right treatment.

How to Prepare

A midstream urine sample is collected in a sterile container to prevent contamination for accurate results.

Understanding Results

Results indicate whether bacteria are present and which antibiotics are most effective for treatment.
