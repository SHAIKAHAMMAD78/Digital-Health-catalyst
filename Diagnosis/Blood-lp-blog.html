Lipid Panel Test

What is a Lipid Panel Test?

The Lipid Panel Test measures cholesterol and triglyceride levels in the blood to assess heart disease risk.

How Does It Work?

A blood sample is taken and analyzed to measure total cholesterol, LDL, HDL, and triglycerides.

Why is It Important?

The test helps detect abnormal cholesterol levels, which can indicate cardiovascular disease risks.

Benefits of Lipid Panel Test

It provides insights into heart health and helps guide lifestyle changes or medication decisions.

How to Prepare

Fasting for 9-12 hours before the test is usually required for accurate results.

Understanding Results

Results show cholesterol levels, helping to assess heart disease risk and inform treatment plans.
