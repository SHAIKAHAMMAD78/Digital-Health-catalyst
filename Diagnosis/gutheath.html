What is Gut health Testing?

Gut health testing evaluates the digestive system, microbiome, and overall gut function. It helps detect issues like infections, inflammation, food intolerances, and digestive disorders. Tests analyze stool, blood, or breath to assess bacteria, enzymes, and nutrient absorption. Poor gut health can lead to bloating, IBS, acid reflux, and other digestive problems. These tests help doctors recommend diet, probiotics, or treatments for better gut health.
