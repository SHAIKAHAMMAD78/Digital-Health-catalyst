Coagulation Panel (Clotting Tests)

What is a Coagulation Panel?

A Coagulation Panel evaluates the bloodâ€™s ability to clot properly and helps diagnose bleeding disorders.

How Does It Work?

A blood sample is taken and tested for clotting factors like PT, INR, and aPTT to assess clotting function.

Why is It Important?

These tests help diagnose clotting disorders, monitor blood-thinning medications, and detect potential health risks.

Benefits of Coagulation Panel

Early detection of clotting issues helps prevent serious conditions like stroke, deep vein thrombosis, and excessive bleeding.

How to Prepare

Fasting is not required, but inform your doctor about any medications that may affect clotting results.
