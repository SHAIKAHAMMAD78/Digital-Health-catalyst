Iron Tests

What are Iron Tests?

Iron tests measure the amount of iron in your blood to diagnose conditions like anemia or iron overload.

How Do They Work?

A blood sample is analyzed to assess iron levels, including ferritin, transferrin, and total iron-binding capacity.

Why are They Important?

Iron is essential for oxygen transport in the blood. Deficiencies or excess levels can cause serious health issues.

Benefits of These Tests

Iron tests help diagnose anemia, monitor iron therapy, and detect iron overload conditions like hemochromatosis.

How to Prepare

Fasting may be required before the test. Avoid iron supplements for at least 24 hours prior to testing.

Understanding Results

Results indicate iron deficiency, normal levels, or excess iron, helping doctors plan appropriate treatment.
