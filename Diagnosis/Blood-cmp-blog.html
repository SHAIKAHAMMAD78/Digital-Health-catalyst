Comprehensive Metabolic Panel (CMP)

What is a Comprehensive Metabolic Panel (CMP)?

The Comprehensive Metabolic Panel (CMP) is a blood test that provides critical information about your body's metabolism, including liver and kidney function, electrolyte levels, and blood sugar.

How Does It Work?

A blood sample is drawn and analyzed to measure substances such as glucose, calcium, protein, and liver enzymes.

Why is It Important?

The CMP test helps diagnose conditions like liver disease, kidney disease, diabetes, and electrolyte imbalances that affect overall health.

Benefits of CMP Test

The test helps in monitoring chronic conditions, assessing organ function, and guiding treatment plans.
