What is Diabetes Testing?

Diabetes testing measures blood sugar (glucose) levels to diagnose and monitor diabetes. It helps detect Type 1, Type 2, and gestational diabetes. The tests check fasting, random, and long-term glucose levels in the blood. Regular testing is crucial for early detection and management of diabetes. These tests help doctors plan treatment and lifestyle changes.
