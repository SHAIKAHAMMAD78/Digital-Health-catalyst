What is Urine Testing?

Urine testing is a diagnostic tool used to detect infections, kidney diseases, diabetes, and metabolic disorders. Common tests include urinalysis, urine culture, and tests for glucose, protein, ketones, and pH levels. Specialized tests help diagnose conditions like pregnancy, drug use, kidney stones, and liver disorders. A 24-hour urine test measures kidney function and detects hormone levels. These tests provide valuable insights into overall health and disease management.
