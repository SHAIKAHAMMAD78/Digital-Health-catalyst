Specific IgE Blood Test

What is a Specific IgE Blood Test?

The Specific IgE Blood Test is a diagnostic test used to measure the level of IgE antibodies in the blood to detect allergies.

How Does It Work?

A blood sample is taken and analyzed to detect the presence of specific IgE antibodies to different allergens.

Why is It Important?

This test helps diagnose allergies without exposing individuals to potential allergens, making it safer than skin tests.

Benefits of Specific IgE Test

Provides precise allergy detection, useful for individuals with skin conditions or those unable to undergo skin tests.

How to Prepare

No special preparation is needed, but it's advised to inform your doctor about any medications you're taking.

Understanding Results

Results indicate the presence and severity of allergies, guiding doctors in creating personalized treatment plans.
