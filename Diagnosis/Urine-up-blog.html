Urine Protein Test (Proteinuria Test)

What is a Urine Protein Test?

A urine protein test measures the amount of protein in the urine to detect kidney issues.

How Does It Work?

A urine sample is analyzed for protein levels, which may indicate kidney damage or disease.

Why is It Important?

This test helps detect early kidney disease, allowing timely medical intervention.

Benefits of Urine Protein Test

It helps monitor kidney health and detect conditions such as diabetes and hypertension-related kidney damage.

How to Prepare

Generally, no special preparation is needed, but hydration is recommended before giving a sample.

Understanding Results

Results indicate protein levels in urine, which can help assess kidney function and disease risk.
