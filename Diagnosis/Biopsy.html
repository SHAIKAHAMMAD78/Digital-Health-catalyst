What is Biopsy Testing?

A biopsy is a medical test where a small tissue sample is taken for examination under a microscope. It helps diagnose cancer, infections, and other diseases. The sample is collected using a needle, surgery, or endoscopy. Biopsies can be performed on organs like the skin, liver, lungs, and breast. The results help doctors determine treatment plans and disease severity
