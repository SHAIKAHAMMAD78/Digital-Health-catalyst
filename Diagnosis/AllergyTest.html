What is Allergy Testing ?

Allergy testing aims to identify specific substances, called allergens, that trigger an immune system response in an individual. These tests can involve skin exposure to potential allergens, blood analysis for allergy-related antibodies, or controlled oral challenges. The results help healthcare providers diagnose allergies and develop strategies to manage or avoid allergic reactions.
