Hormone Tests

What are Hormone Tests?

Hormone tests measure levels of various hormones in the blood to diagnose hormonal imbalances.

How Do They Work?

Blood samples are analyzed to check hormone levels such as thyroid, cortisol, estrogen, and testosterone.

Why are They Important?

Hormone tests help diagnose conditions like thyroid disorders, diabetes, and reproductive issues.

Benefits of Hormone Tests

These tests help in managing hormonal imbalances and improving overall health.

How to Prepare

Some tests require fasting or taking the test at specific times of the day.

Understanding Results

Results help doctors determine treatment options for hormone-related conditions.
