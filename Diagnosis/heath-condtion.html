Health Conditions

Ignoring diagnosis can lead to serious health issues. Here are some examples:

Undiagnosed Diabetes

Without proper diagnosis, diabetes can lead to nerve damage, organ failure, blindness, and even amputations.

Late Cancer Detection

Delayed cancer diagnosis reduces treatment effectiveness and increases the risk of fatality.

Heart Diseases

Ignoring symptoms can lead to silent heart attacks, stroke, or sudden cardiac arrest.

Neurological Disorders

Delaying diagnosis of brain conditions can lead to irreversible memory loss and cognitive decline.

Chronic Lung Diseases

Untreated lung conditions like COPD and asthma can cause severe breathing difficulties and lung failure.

Kidney Disease

Chronic kidney disease can lead to kidney failure, requiring dialysis or transplantation.

Osteoporosis

Without early diagnosis, brittle bones increase the risk of fractures, leading to long-term disability.

Mental Health Disorders

Undiagnosed conditions like depression and anxiety can severely impact life quality and lead to suicidal thoughts.
