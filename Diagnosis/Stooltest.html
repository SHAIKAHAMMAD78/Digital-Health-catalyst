What is Stool Testing?

Stool testing is a diagnostic procedure used to analyze stool samples for infections, digestive disorders, and gastrointestinal diseases. It helps detect bacteria, parasites, blood, inflammation, and abnormal enzyme levels. Common tests include stool culture, fecal occult blood test (FOBT), and tests for parasites or digestive malabsorption. These tests aid in diagnosing conditions like food poisoning, inflammatory bowel disease (IBD), colon cancer, and pancreatic disorders. Regular stool analysis is crucial for early detection and effective treatment of gastrointestinal health issues.
