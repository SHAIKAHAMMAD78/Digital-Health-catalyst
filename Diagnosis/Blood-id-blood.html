Infectious Disease Tests

What are Infectious Disease Tests?

Infectious disease tests help detect viruses, bacteria, fungi, and other pathogens that cause diseases.

How Do They Work?

These tests analyze blood, urine, or tissue samples to identify the presence of harmful pathogens.

Why are They Important?

Early detection of infections allows for timely treatment and prevents the spread of diseases.

Benefits of Infectious Disease Tests

These tests help diagnose and manage conditions like COVID-19, HIV, tuberculosis, and more.

How to Prepare

Some tests may require fasting or avoiding certain medications before sample collection.

Understanding Results

Positive results indicate an active or past infection, while negative results suggest no detected pathogens.
