What is Neurological Testing?

Neurological testing assesses the brain, spinal cord, and nerves to diagnose conditions like strokes, epilepsy, multiple sclerosis, and nerve disorders. It includes imaging tests (MRI, CT scan), electrical activity tests (EEG, EMG), and cerebrospinal fluid analysis (lumbar puncture). These tests help identify abnormalities in brain function, nerve damage, or circulation issues. Neurological exams also evaluate cognitive abilities, reflexes, and motor skills. Early diagnosis through these tests improves treatment and management of neurological diseases.
