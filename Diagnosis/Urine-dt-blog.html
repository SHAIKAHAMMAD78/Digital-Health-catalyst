Urine Drug Test

What is a Urine Drug Test?

A urine drug test detects the presence of drugs or their metabolites in urine, commonly used for screening purposes.

How Does It Work?

A urine sample is analyzed for specific substances using immunoassay or chromatography techniques.

Why is It Important?

It is used for workplace testing, medical evaluations, and legal compliance.

Benefits of Urine Drug Test

Provides quick, non-invasive detection of drug use with accurate results.

How to Prepare

Avoiding substances that may cause false positives and following collection guidelines is essential.

Understanding Results

Results indicate the presence of drugs, which may require further confirmatory testing.
