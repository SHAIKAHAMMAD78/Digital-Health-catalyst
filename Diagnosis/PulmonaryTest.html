What is Pulmonary Testing?

Pulmonary testing assesses lung function and helps diagnose respiratory conditions. It includes tests like spirometry, chest X-rays, and arterial blood gas analysis to measure airflow, oxygen levels, and lung capacity. These tests help detect asthma, COPD, lung infections, and other pulmonary diseases. Non-invasive methods like pulse oximetry and imaging scans provide quick assessments, while procedures like bronchoscopy offer detailed lung examination. Pulmonary function tests are essential for monitoring lung health and guiding treatment.
