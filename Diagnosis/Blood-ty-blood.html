Thyroid Function Tests

What are Thyroid Function Tests?

Thyroid Function Tests help assess the health of the thyroid gland by measuring hormone levels in the blood.

How Does It Work?

A blood sample is taken to measure levels of TSH, T3, and T4 hormones to evaluate thyroid activity.

Why is It Important?

These tests help diagnose thyroid disorders such as hypothyroidism and hyperthyroidism.

Benefits of Thyroid Function Tests

They help monitor treatment effectiveness and detect thyroid imbalances early.

How to Prepare

No special preparation is usually needed, but some medications may interfere with results.

Understanding Results

Results show thyroid hormone levels and help guide treatment decisions for thyroid conditions.
