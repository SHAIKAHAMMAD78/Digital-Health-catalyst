What is Blood Testing?

Blood testing is a medical procedure where a sample of blood is analyzed to assess overall health, detect diseases, monitor organ function, and evaluate the effectiveness of treatments. It helps diagnose conditions like infections, anemia, diabetes, and cholesterol levels. Common tests include complete blood count (CBC), blood sugar tests, and lipid profiles. It is a quick, reliable way to gain insights into a person's health.
