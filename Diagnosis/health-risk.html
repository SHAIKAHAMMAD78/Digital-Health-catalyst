Health Risks of Various Body Parts

Brain

Health risks: Stroke, Alzheimer's, Parkinson's, migraines. Maintain brain health with a balanced diet and regular exercise.

Heart

Health risks: Heart disease, hypertension, cholesterol issues. Regular physical activity and a healthy diet reduce risks.

Lungs

Health risks: Asthma, COPD, lung cancer. Avoid smoking and pollutants for better lung health.

Liver

Health risks: Fatty liver, hepatitis, cirrhosis. Limit alcohol and eat a liver-friendly diet.

Kidneys

Health risks: Kidney stones, kidney failure. Stay hydrated and maintain balanced sodium intake.

Stomach

Health risks: Gastritis, acid reflux, ulcers. Avoid processed foods and manage stress.
